Frequently Asked Questions

Is Happy Vegas free to use?
Yes! Happy Vegas is free to download and use. The app is supported by ads, and you can purchase a one-time Remove Ads upgrade for $4.99 if you prefer an ad-free experience.
How do I save my favorite spots?
Sign in with your Apple ID to sync your favorites across all your devices. Tap the heart icon on any happy hour, restaurant, or nightlife venue to add it to your favorites list.
How often are listings updated?
We regularly update our listings to keep prices, hours, and details accurate. Vegas changes fast, and we do our best to keep up. If you notice something outdated, please let us know.
A happy hour deal or listing is wrong. How do I report it?
We take accuracy seriously. Email us at Happylasvegas702@gmail.com with the venue name, what's incorrect, and the correct info if you have it. We'll get it fixed as soon as possible.
Can I request a new venue be added?
Absolutely! Send us an email with the venue name, location, and any happy hour or deal details you know about, and we'll look into adding it.
How do I restore my Remove Ads purchase on a new device?
Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases." As long as you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase, your ad-free experience will be restored at no additional cost.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from the Profile screen within the app using the "Clear Profile" option. This will remove your profile, favorites, and all associated data. If you need further assistance, contact us at Happylasvegas702@gmail.com.
